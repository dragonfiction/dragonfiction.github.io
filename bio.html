Who's Who?

Aron Preece / "Noswaith"

Aron Preece is the founder of Dragon Fiction and the head author of Ceidwaid. Born in South Wales in the late 90s, Aron picked up the electronic pencil and started writing amateurishly in late 2013, he later refined his writing style in the middle of 2015 to create the young adult story of Ceidwaid.

While he admits he's far from any degree of professional, he is happy with his progress thus far and looks to continue improving.

"Hydref"

The second addition to the team, Hydref originates from the south of Washington State and was also born in the late 90s. He joined the team in January 2016.

He mainly favours Adventure stories and enjoys Egyptian mythology.

"Wawr"

The third addition to the team, Wawr was born in the mid 90s in London. He joined the team in January 2016.

Determined, he seeks to try and write a plethora of story genres to expand the collect here at Dragon Fiction.
