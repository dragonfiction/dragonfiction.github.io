Forger of Rome: My thoughts and apologies. 16/04/2016

Sorry.

I really didn't mean for it to be this long before finishing the story. Truth is, I got bored with it really quickly and lost motivation to work on it. I'm aware there's also some historical fluff-ups. To which I'll address below.

Spoilers await!

Okay so, firstly - Octavius in real life became Augustus in 27 B.C. However, in the story this happens in 30 B.C. right after the battle of Alexandria.

Secondly, Mark Antony. Some records I read have him dying after his defeat at the Battle of Actium. Others suggest his death was after Alexandria was taken. For creative reasons, I've used this for the story.
