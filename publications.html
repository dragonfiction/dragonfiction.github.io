Ceidwaid

Brief

Ceidwaid is based on a group of angelic immortal warriors, as they battle Demonic forces to keep the balance of good and evil. It's comprised of both the sci-fi genre and action genre, with several side stories focusing on a different character and genre. Ceidwaid is the Welsh word for 'keepers, rangers' and it follows a comic book like style of frequent, short issues.
